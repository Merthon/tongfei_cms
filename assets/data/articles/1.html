Chengdu, China — According to preliminary estimates, China’s nuclear technology application industry is approaching RMB 900 billion in scale by the end of 2024.

With an average annual growth rate exceeding 15%, the market is expected to surpass RMB 1 trillion in 2025 and could potentially double by 2030. These figures highlight the rapid expansion of the sector and signal that nuclear technology applications are moving from a niche field into a major driver of the national economy.

The 2025 International Conference on Nuclear Technology Applications, hosted by the China Nuclear Energy Industry Association, opened on October 15 in Chengdu. Tonfy was invited to participate, presenting its nuclear-focused thermal management solutions and officially announcing the nuclear energy sector as a strategic area for its future growth.

Precision Cooling for High-Tech Nuclear Applications

Since 2017, Tonfy has maintained a deep collaboration with the China National Nuclear Corporation (CNNC), establishing a strong presence in the nuclear sector. Nuclear technology applications require extremely high operational stability.

Core components needing ±0.1°C temperature control to ensure reliable system performance.

Leveraging over 20 years of expertise in industrial temperature control, Tonfy has developed high-efficiency heat exchange modules combined with intelligent closed-loop control systems, successfully addressing the cooling challenges of high-power density equipment. These innovations provide stable, reliable thermal management for nuclear devices and lay a solid foundation for Tonfy’s continued expansion in the nuclear sector.
