Product Features

The all-new Mate Cool laser water chiller has four core features: comprehensive structural upgrade, low operating noise, high-precision temperature control, and zero water leakage risk. At the same time, it can provide customized temperature control solutions to meet the cooling needs of laser equipment with different powers and processes.

1. Comprehensive Structural Upgrade

To address the issue of product space utilization, the layout of the equipment's water inlets/outlets and air inlets/outlets has been optimized. On the premise of ensuring the product's heat exchange performance, one side of the product is designed as a full sheet metal surface, enabling the equipment to be installed against the wall. Meanwhile, the internal structure of the product adopts a highly integrated design, saving 30% of space for users compared with the previous generation. This not only facilitates flexible deployment in limited spaces, but also reduces transportation and installation costs, providing great convenience for users.

2. Low Equipment Operating Noise

During the design process of this product, high-quality low-noise fans are carefully selected. Through precise acoustic design and optimization of air flow channels, noise is reduced to the greatest extent. After strict simulation tests and model experiments, the low-noise operation of the equipment is finally achieved.

3. High-Precision Temperature Control

The precision and sensitivity of temperature control equipment are the most intuitive manifestations of equipment performance. Relying on the company's more than 20 years of experience in R&D of temperature control equipment, many technological achievements have been applied to the Mate Cool product, enabling it to achieve high-precision control of water temperature. At the same time, it is equipped with real-time monitoring and automatic water temperature adjustment functions to ensure that the laser always works within the optimal temperature range, thereby extending its service life and improving processing precision.

4. Zero Water Leakage Electric Water Pump

To completely eliminate the water leakage hidden danger of traditional water pumps, the all-new Mate Cool laser water chiller innovatively adopts electric water pump technology. This type of water pump not only has a higher energy efficiency ratio, but also ensures long-term operational stability and reliability through sealing design and intelligent monitoring functions, effectively avoiding equipment damage and production interruption caused by water leakage.
