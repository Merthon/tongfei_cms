In April 1–5, 2024, the highly anticipated Seoul International Manufacturing Technology Show (SIMTOS) 2024 was grandly held at KINTEX.

At this global gathering of advanced manufacturing technologies, TONFY once again demonstrated its strong capabilities in industrial temperature control and innovative product solutions on the international stage.

As one of the most representative exhibitions in Korea’s machine tool industry, SIMTOS 2024 is hosted by the Korea Machine Tool Manufacturers' Association and supported by several key institutions. The exhibition serves as an important platform that reflects the development trends of the global machine tool industry.

Diverse Thermal Management for Global Scenarios

At the exhibition, TONFY presented a range of temperature control products designed for equipment manufacturing and energy storage applications, offering comprehensive thermal management solutions for diverse industrial scenarios.

The exhibit highlighted the company’s well-established product portfolio and attracted numerous industry professionals. TONFY’s professional team engaged with visitors, providing detailed introductions to product performance and the latest developments in temperature control technologies.

Expanding the Footprint of Intelligent Manufacturing

Through continuous technological innovation and consistent improvements in product quality, TONFY has earned the trust of international customers. During SIMTOS 2024, TONFY’s products were showcased in a variety of real-world application scenarios for global clients.
