Seoul, Korea — In April 1–5, 2024, the highly anticipated Seoul International Manufacturing Technology Show (SIMTOS) 2024 was grandly held at KINTEX.

At this global gathering of advanced manufacturing technologies, TONFY once again demonstrated its strong capabilities in industrial temperature control and innovative product solutions on the international stage.

As one of the most representative exhibitions in Korea’s machine tool industry, SIMTOS 2024 is hosted by the Korea Machine Tool Manufacturers' Association and supported by several key institutions, including the Ministry of Science and ICT. The exhibition serves as an important platform that reflects the development trends of the global machine tool industry.

Diverse Thermal Management Solutions

At the exhibition, TONFY presented a range of temperature control products designed for equipment manufacturing and energy storage applications, offering comprehensive thermal management solutions for diverse industrial scenarios.

The exhibit highlighted the company’s well-established product portfolio. TONFY’s professional team engaged with visitors, providing detailed introductions to product performance, application scenarios, and the latest developments in temperature control technologies across various industries.

Global Presence and Quality Innovation

In recent years, TONFY has achieved remarkable success in the domestic market while actively expanding its global presence. Through continuous technological innovation and consistent improvements in product quality, the company has earned the trust of international customers.

TONFY’s impressive performance at SIMTOS 2024 not only demonstrated its strong expertise in temperature control solutions for equipment manufacturing, but also showcased the growing strength of Chinese intelligent manufacturing on the global stage.

Moving forward, TONFY will continue to upgrade its products and technologies to ensure reliable and stable operation for industrial equipment across multiple sectors.
