Focusing on the semiconductor manufacturing industry’s stringent requirements for heat management fluids, TONFY has developed high-precision fluorinated liquid cooling units to solve critical thermal challenges.

The launch of these units injects innovative capabilities into the temperature control requirements of the semiconductor industry, significantly enhancing production efficiency and product quality for the sustainable development of "China Chip".

Key Technological Advantages

  • Ultra-High Precision: Equipped with 16-bit high-resolution AD conversion, the temperature resolution reaches 0.001°C, ensuring extreme stability throughout the manufacturing process.
  • Extended Temperature Range: Reliable operation from -20°C to +90°C, accommodating a wider range of critical manufacturing environments.
  • Optimized PID Algorithm: Temperature accuracy reaches ±0.02°C through industry-leading control logic.
  • Wide Application Scope: Suitable for semiconductor manufacturing, instrumentation, CNC machine tools, and laser processing.

Supported by TONFY’s strong R&D capabilities — over 250 engineering staff and advanced simulation capabilities — the company continuously enhances customized solutions for the most demanding technical environments.

World-Class Testing Infrastructure

TONFY maintains ten specialized internal laboratories, including climate simulation and semi-anechoic chambers. In 2023, the product testing center obtained CNAS accreditation , establishing a comprehensive and precise inspection system.

This robust technical platform ensures every step from design to finished product meets the highest standards, providing a strong foundation for continuous innovation in the "China Chip" ecosystem.
