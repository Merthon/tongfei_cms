From June 14–16, 2024, the ees Europe 2024 was grandly held at the New Munich Trade Fair Center in Germany.

As a leading Chinese manufacturer of thermal management equipment for the energy storage industry, TONFY leveraged its strong domestic foundation and global vision to participate in this international event, presenting a series of air-cooled and liquid-cooled energy storage solutions.

A Global Hub for Energy Innovation

The exhibition attracted over 80,000 visitors from 160 countries. As part of "The smarter E Europe," ees Europe has grown rapidly, covering more than 45,000 m² this year. TONFY's booth became a focal point for manufacturers, plant planners, and project developers seeking reliable thermal stability.

At the exhibition, TONFY showcased three distinct energy storage thermal management systems, demonstrating the strength of "Made in China" to the international market and attracting wide attention from industry professionals.

Driving a World-Class Global Presence

Europe represents a massive market for both centralized and distributed energy storage. Beyond Europe, TONFY is rapidly expanding in Southeast Asia, Japan, South Korea, and Australia to meet the rising demand driven by energy security.

Global Vision

Committed to becoming a world-class industrial thermal management expert, TONFY continues to expand its reach and create value for global customers.

This exhibition marks another successful step in TONFY’s international journey, heralding a new era of overseas development and market reach.
