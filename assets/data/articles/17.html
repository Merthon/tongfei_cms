On June 18, 2024, TONFY’s German subsidiary, ATF Refrigeration GmbH , hosted an exclusive appreciation dinner atop the iconic Stuttgart TV Tower.

The event, themed “See You Again on Top of Stuttgart,” brought together representatives from industry giants including EMAG, TRUMPF, CHIRON, WEINIG, Schenck Ro Tec , and Christian Maier to celebrate long-term cooperation and future innovation.

A Decade of Growth in Germany

During the evening, Mr. Zhang Haolei, General Manager of TONFY, reflected on the company's eleven-year journey in Germany. Since establishing ATF as a wholly-owned subsidiary in Stuttgart in 2017, the brand has solidified its presence as a trusted local partner.

“Our relationships are built on more than a decade of shared success,” Mr. Zhang stated. He emphasized that TONFY and ATF will continue to harmonize developments from China and Germany while maintaining a laser focus on the global industrial market.

Global Vision: Creating Customer Value

TONFY’s “Going Global” strategy has successfully brought Chinese innovation to Europe, Southeast Asia, and North America. By adhering to the mission of “creating value for customers,” TONFY is not just a supplier but a strategic partner to world-renowned enterprises.

Stronger Together

Moving forward, TONFY and ATF will continue to foster joint development with partners, strengthening their position as a respected global leader in industrial temperature control.

The night concluded with a warm invitation from Mr. Zhang to all partners: “Welcome to China!” — signaling a future of even deeper cross-border collaboration.
