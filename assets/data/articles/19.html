From September 10–12, 2024, TONFY crossed the Pacific to present its vision for green energy at RE+ 2024 , held at the Anaheim Convention Center in California.

As a premier global event for renewable energy and energy storage, RE+ provided the perfect stage for TONFY to showcase its latest advancements in thermal management solutions to the North American market and beyond.

Innovation in Focus: Reliable Energy Solutions

At the exhibition, TONFY’s comprehensive energy storage thermal management solutions became a primary highlight. Specifically designed for large-scale energy storage applications, the equipment impressed attendees with its:

  • High Energy Efficiency: Optimized cooling cycles to reduce auxiliary power consumption.
  • Stable Operation: Proven reliability in various climate conditions across the US.
  • Strategic Design: Compact and easy-to-integrate units for modern energy containers.

Deepening Partnerships for Carbon Neutrality

During the three-day event, TONFY successfully reached strategic cooperation intentions with several renowned international enterprises. These partnerships are set to accelerate TONFY's global deployment and contribute to the collective goal of achieving global carbon neutrality.

A Greener Tomorrow

TONFY remains committed to providing efficient, reliable, and sustainable thermal management solutions to advance the clean energy era alongside our global partners.

As the exhibition concluded, TONFY reinforced its commitment to the global energy storage sector, looking forward to creating a greener and more sustainable future together.
