Singapore — The Singapore leg of Data Centre World (DCW), the global annual benchmark for the data center industry, officially opened on October 8 at the Marina Bay Sands Expo & Convention Centre.

As a key Asia-Pacific platform for data center innovation, the event attracted over 200 exhibitors and 8,000 visitors, focusing on the theme of a sustainable future in the AI era.

Tonfy made a powerful appearance, showcasing its full portfolio of data center thermal management solutions and demonstrating the innovation and engineering excellence of Chinese manufacturing to the international market.

Product Launches Driving Technological Innovation

As the data center industry enters the AI computing boom, power per rack has surged from traditional 50 kW to several hundred kilowatts, particularly with large-scale high-density GPU clusters. This exponential increase in compute density is driving a critical demand for high-performance cooling solutions.

Conventional air-cooled systems struggle to meet these high-density requirements due to limitations in heat dissipation and energy efficiency. Liquid cooling has shifted from being an optional solution to an essential technology.

At DCW Singapore, Tonfy unveiled two new CDU products:

  • 2.6MW Cabinet CDU
  • 150kW Liquid-to-Liquid Rack CDU

These products target two core demands: high-density compute adaptation and industry-standard deployment. With differentiated architectures, they offer a tiered solution covering scenarios from small-to-medium density edge deployments to ultra-large-scale clusters, delivering performance aligned with industry benchmarks and providing precise cooling solutions for a range of computing environments.

Full-Matrix Product Line Demonstrates Technical Leadership

In addition to the new CDUs, Tonfy also showcased:

  • 20kW Air-to-Liquid Rack CDU (stainless steel heat exchanger)
  • 70kW Mini Air Wall
  • Micro-module models
  • Integrated cooling station models
  • Manifold solutions

This comprehensive display demonstrates Tonfy’s full-matrix, full-scenario coverage in the data center sector, highlighting the company’s deep technical expertise and innovation in thermal management.

Expanding Overseas with Sustainable Solutions

Looking ahead, Tonfy will actively expand into the overseas data center market, leveraging its Singapore subsidiary to serve the Southeast Asia region. With a focus on high-efficiency, stable, and environmentally friendly products, supported by fast and reliable after-sales service, Tonfy aims to inject green energy into global data center operations and contribute to a sustainable digital economy.

Through this exhibition, Tonfy not only showcased Chinese innovation in data center thermal management to the international market but also laid a solid foundation for further expansion in Southeast Asia. As global digitization accelerates, Tonfy will continue to drive innovation and deliver cutting-edge thermal management solutions to the worldwide data center industry.
