Technical Deep Dive: Four Core Solutions

  • Centralized Liquid-Cooling: Replaces seven individual units with one central unit, reducing components by 30% and cutting energy consumption by 15%.
  • Full-Stack Liquid-Cooling: Combines PACK + PCS dual cooling, boosting annual efficiency by 30% through integrated natural cooling.
  • Immersion Liquid-Cooling: Battery cells fully immersed in non-flammable coolant, reducing thermal resistance by 50% and extending life by 15%.
  • Direct-Cooling & Heating: Refrigerant directly reaches cold plates, increasing COP by 12% and replacing electric heating with heat pumps to save 40% energy in winter.
