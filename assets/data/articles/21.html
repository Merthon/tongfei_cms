On September 10, 2024, the Stuttgart Metalworking Exhibition (AMB 2024) opened at the New Stuttgart Trade Fair Center.

TONFY, together with its wholly-owned German subsidiary ATF Refrigeration GmbH , showcased over two decades of technological expertise. As a premier event for the metalworking industry, AMB provided a vital platform for TONFY to present its latest innovations to a highly professional European audience.

Environmentally Friendly Refrigerant Technologies

In response to the EU’s newly proposed F-gas regulations , which mandate lower Global Warming Potential (GWP) values, TONFY highlighted its commitment to sustainable manufacturing. The company showcased units utilizing eco-friendly refrigerants including:

  • R290, R32, and R513A: Solutions with GWP ≤ 750.
  • Sustainable Compliance: Future-proofing thermal management for the European market.
  • Precision Control: High-performance cooling even with green refrigerant alternatives.

Expanding Global Market Reach

Participation in AMB 2024 enabled the TONFY team to connect with key potential partners in the machine tool and metalworking sectors. By deepening strategic cooperation with global partners and increasing R&D investment, TONFY continues to drive the transition toward intelligent and green manufacturing .

Innovation & Collaboration

TONFY remains dedicated to providing high-quality, efficient, and reliable solutions that meet the stringent environmental and technical demands of the global stage.

As the exhibition concluded, TONFY reinforced its role as an innovation-driven leader, ready to seize new opportunities in the international manufacturing landscape.
