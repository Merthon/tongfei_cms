As part of TONFY’s global brand strategy, Amada (China) Co., Ltd. officially recognized TONFY as a “Model Factory for Sheet Metal Smart Manufacturing” on August 31, 2023.

This recognition and the subsequent signing of a strategic cooperation agreement further consolidate the long-term collaboration between TONFY and Japan’s Amada Corporation, a top-tier global leader in sheet metal processing equipment.

Precision Meets Innovation

TONFY has consistently integrated Amada’s advanced sheet metal equipment into its production lines. This investment has significantly improved:

  • Processing Precision: Achieving tighter tolerances for high-end thermal management enclosures.
  • Manufacturing Efficiency: Streamlining the production of complex industrial cooling systems.
  • Resource Optimization: Leveraging Amada’s smart technology to reduce material waste and energy consumption.

A Renewed Global Vision

With this renewed strategic cooperation, both parties will engage in joint technology innovation, product development, and market expansion. By sharing resources and complementing each other's technical advantages, TONFY and Amada aim to address global market challenges together.

Professionalism Creates Value

This partnership enhances TONFY's global competitiveness and reinforces its commitment to delivering outstanding products to the equipment manufacturing industry.

Looking ahead, TONFY will continue to uphold its guiding principles, ensuring a first-class customer service system and contributing to the advancement of high-quality industrial manufacturing worldwide.
