On May 20, 2024, the 12th Busan International Machinery Exhibition (BUTECH) officially opened at the Busan Exhibition & Convention Center, South Korea.

As a leading provider of industrial thermal management equipment, TONFY showcased a lineup of flagship products, drawing significant attention from the global manufacturing sector. BUTECH stands as Asia’s premier industrial machinery exhibition, bringing together the region's top innovators and decision-makers.

Efficiency and Reliability in Focus

TONFY presented a range of high-efficiency and energy-saving thermal management solutions tailored for equipment manufacturing. These solutions are designed to maintain peak performance in demanding industrial environments:

  • High-Precision Control: Ensuring stability for complex CNC and automated machinery.
  • Energy Saving: Advanced refrigeration cycles that minimize operational costs.
  • Global Compliance: Products designed to meet rigorous international quality and safety standards.

Accelerating Global Strategy

Participation in BUTECH 2024 has further enhanced TONFY’s brand recognition in East Asia. By leveraging strong technical capabilities and comprehensive after-sales support, TONFY continues to establish a robust global sales and service network.

Smarter Industrial Solutions

Looking ahead, TONFY will continue to drive product upgrades through innovation, providing the global equipment manufacturing industry with higher-quality thermal management solutions.

The successful event in Busan marks another milestone in TONFY's journey to support high-quality industrial development worldwide through advanced thermal technology.
