The EMO Hannover 2023, one of the world’s most influential machine tool exhibitions, officially opened on September 18, 2023, at the Hannover Exhibition Center in Germany.

TONFY, together with its wholly owned German subsidiary ATF, made a strong appearance, presenting intelligent thermal management solutions to over 1,700 well-known companies and professional visitors from across the globe.

A Comprehensive Portfolio for Intelligent Manufacturing

As precision requirements for machine tools reach new heights, TONFY’s R&D capabilities have evolved to provide ultra-precise temperature control. At the exhibition, the company highlighted several core products:

  • Cutting Fluid Chillers: Specifically designed for stable machining environments.
  • Variable-Frequency Water Chillers: Offering high-efficiency cooling with adaptive control.
  • Industrial Air Conditioners: Ensuring reliability for sensitive control cabinets.
  • Specialized Units: Including Oil Coolers, Laser Water Chillers, and Air-to-Water Heat Exchangers.

Ten Years of International Excellence

TONFY first participated in EMO Hannover in 2013, which marked its first step into the global arena. Returning in 2023, TONFY demonstrated how it has grown from an emerging player into a leading global provider of industrial thermal management solutions, now serving sectors from energy storage to power electronics.

Creating Value Globally

With over 20 years of expertise, TONFY remains committed to contributing to the global advancement of intelligent manufacturing through reliable and innovative technology.

Throughout September 2023, TONFY’s diversified strategy was on full display as it also participated in the MWC CNC Machine Tool Exhibition in Shanghai, further solidifying its position as a market leader in multiple regions.
