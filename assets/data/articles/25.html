Amid the rapid growth of the global energy storage industry, TONFY has achieved a major milestone: its liquid-cooling solution for high-voltage cascaded energy storage systems has exceeded 2 GWh in total installed capacity .

Leveraging over 20 years of expertise, TONFY has developed a series of efficient thermal management solutions. Among these, the High-Voltage Cascaded Direct-Connected solution stands out for its ability to eliminate step-up transformers and maximize energy efficiency.

Overcoming Key Technical Challenges

High-voltage cascaded systems present unique thermal and electrical challenges. TONFY's independently developed solution effectively addresses:

  • Low Electrical Conductivity: Specialized coolant management to prevent electrical leakage in high-voltage environments.
  • Low-Noise Operation: Advanced acoustic design for flexible deployment near commercial areas.
  • Superior Uniformity: Precise temperature balancing to ensure battery cell longevity and safety.
