On the dynamic industrial stage of Türkiye, WIN Eurasia stands out as a major event for global manufacturers. The exhibition, which concluded on May 31, brought together the latest trends in smart manufacturing and energy technology.

As a leading provider of thermal management solutions, TONFY actively participated in this premier event, leveraging Türkiye’s strategic position as a key industrial hub bridging Europe and Asia to expand its global footprint along the Belt and Road Initiative route.

Six Key Sectors & TONFY's Synergy

WIN Eurasia focused on six major industrial sectors, all of which align closely with TONFY’s core temperature control expertise:

  • Industrial Automation: High-precision cooling for CNC machine tools and robotics.
  • Energy Technologies: Thermal management for hydrogen systems and smart grids.
  • Electrical & Electronics: Specialized cooling for EV components and storage batteries.
  • Green Manufacturing: Sustainable solutions for solar power and recycling equipment.

Connecting Technology with Global Partnerships

Participation in WIN Eurasia provided an ideal platform to demonstrate TONFY’s advanced technologies while seeking strategic partnerships in Türkiye. TONFY believes that close cooperation with local partners will lead to resource sharing, complementary strengths, and mutual success in these rapidly developing markets.

A Collaborative Ecosystem

TONFY aims to build a win-win industrial ecosystem across Eurasia, promoting technological innovation and contributing to global industrial growth.

By integrating resources from international trade agreements and strengthening global partnerships, TONFY continues to reinforce its position as a global leader in the industrial temperature control industry.
