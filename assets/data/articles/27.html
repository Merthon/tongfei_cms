As the global energy transition accelerates, hydrogen energy is emerging as a clean force shaping the future. TONFY is leveraging its technological innovation to inject new momentum into this vital industry.

The rapid advancement of hydrogen relies on stable thermal management. TONFY provides full-chain solutions—from production and storage to transportation and application—enhancing system efficiency and operational safety across the entire value chain.

Core Applications in the Hydrogen Value Chain

TONFY’s specialized temperature control systems are integrated into key equipment essential for the hydrogen economy:

  • Hydrogen Production: Efficient cooling for power supplies and electrolyzers.
  • Storage & Transport: Thermal regulation for high-pressure hydrogen compressors.
  • Refueling & Purification: Maintaining stability in hydrogen refueling and purification systems.
  • Power Application: Advanced thermal management for high-performance fuel cells.

Pioneering Green Hydrogen Breakthroughs

Notably, TONFY participated in China’s first domestically developed megawatt-level AEM water electrolysis hydrogen production system . As the thermal management provider for this landmark project by Wolong Ennered, TONFY delivered comprehensive solutions that mark a significant step for green hydrogen advancement.
