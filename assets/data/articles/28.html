From October 22 to 25, 2024, the world-renowned EURO BLECH 2024 was grandly held at the Hannover Exhibition Center, Germany.

TONFY, in collaboration with its German subsidiary ATF , presented a powerful display of thermal management technologies specifically designed for the sheet metal processing and equipment manufacturing industries. As one of the most influential global exhibitions in the sector, EURO BLECH served as a vital stage for TONFY's international growth.

A Strong Competitive Edge in Smart Manufacturing

Beyond its own booth, TONFY’s presence was felt throughout the exhibition hall. The company’s thermal management products were featured as integral components within the machinery of numerous domestic and international exhibitors.

This widespread integration demonstrates TONFY's reputation for reliability and its ability to meet the stringent technical requirements of top-tier smart equipment manufacturers worldwide.

  • Global Integration: Trusted as a built-in cooling partner for industry-leading machinery.
  • Customized Solutions: Tailored thermal management for diverse sheet metal working needs.
  • Expert Consultation: Engaging with global clients on future development strategies.

Strengthening Connections with Global Markets

EURO BLECH 2024 allowed TONFY to reinforce its technological capabilities while deepening its bond with the international market. By consistently investing in R&D, TONFY is helping to drive the evolution of global equipment manufacturing.
