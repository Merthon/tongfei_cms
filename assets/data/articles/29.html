On June 9, 2025, People’s Daily published a featured report highlighting the Frankfurt Technology Fair, specifically spotlighting Sanhe TONFY Refrigeration Co., Ltd.

The 2025 Frankfurt Technology Fair, held at the Frankfurt Exhibition Center in Germany, served as a global stage for over 300 leading companies in Big Data, Artificial Intelligence, and Cloud Computing. For TONFY, this was a landmark event—marking its first major appearance in the international data center sector.

A New Era of Data Center Solutions

As AI and Big Data continue to drive global digital transformation, the demand for efficient cooling has never been higher. TONFY utilized this platform to showcase its specialized research and expertise in:

  • Precision Cooling: Advanced thermal management for high-density computing racks.
  • Energy Efficiency: Cutting-edge liquid cooling and air conditioning for sustainable data centers.
  • AI-Driven Control: Smart temperature monitoring systems for uninterrupted operation.
