As a pioneer in industrial thermal management, TONFY has launched the SWW Series , a next-generation water-cooled chiller specifically engineered for semiconductor manufacturing.

Designed for multi-stage operations such as thin-film deposition, this new series addresses the stringent precision and reliability requirements of one of the world's most demanding high-tech industries.

Unmatched Precision & Performance

The SWW Series provides a stable cooling source essential for enhancing process stability and production efficiency:

  • High Accuracy: Temperature control precision of ±0.3°C, ideal for thin-film deposition.
  • Wide Operating Range: Fluid temperature range of 20–90°C and ambient adaptability from 5–45°C.
  • SEMI Standard Compliance: Designed to meet global semiconductor equipment safety and performance standards.

Minimalist Engineering, Maximal Reliability

The chiller features a screwless exterior design, giving it a sleek appearance while minimizing failure risks associated with loose fasteners. This optimized internal layout facilitates easier maintenance and cleaning, significantly reducing long-term operational costs for cleanroom environments.

Expanding the Semiconductor Matrix

TONFY plans to continue expanding its portfolio to include dual-channel chillers and fluorinated liquid chillers, supporting the entire semiconductor manufacturing chain.

The SWW Series has already received high praise from initial customers for its stability and precision, laying a solid foundation for TONFY's future as a key player in high-end semiconductor thermal management.
