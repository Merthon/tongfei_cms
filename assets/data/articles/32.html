On June 12, 2024, ATF Cooling GmbH , TONFY’s wholly-owned German subsidiary, played a key role in coordinating a high-level delegation visit to the VDW (German Machine Tool Builders’ Association) in Frankfurt.

As a trusted supplier to China’s national machine tool team—General Technology Group Machine Tool Co., Ltd.—ATF Managing Director Li Yangbo accompanied a senior leadership delegation, including Chairman Dr. Zhou Zhou, to engage in strategic discussions with VDW CEO Dr. Markus Heering.

Strategic Dialogue on Industry Challenges

The talks focused on the current status of the German machine tool industry and the strategic layout of General Technology Group. Key topics included:

  • Technological Innovation: Jointly addressing global industry challenges.
  • Market Expansion: Discussing the potential of global markets and the importance of localized adaptation.
  • Talent Development: Exploring dual-track vocational education cooperation.
  • Aftermarket Growth: Analyzing the shift toward services as a new growth engine.

Empowering the Global Ecosystem

This visit has laid a solid foundation for future collaboration in machine tool technology and industrial chain integration. As a provider of integrated industrial thermal management solutions, TONFY & ATF remain committed to empowering the high-end manufacturing ecosystem.

Global Vision

"This high-level dialogue strengthens our overseas business footprint and reinforces our competitiveness through diversified market strategies." — ATF Leadership.

Looking ahead, TONFY & ATF will continue to strengthen their role as a bridge between the Chinese and European machine tool markets, driving innovation and sustainable growth for the global industry.
