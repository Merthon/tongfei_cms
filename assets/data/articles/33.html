In the semiconductor industry, as chip fabrication becomes increasingly precise, TONFY has launched a new-generation dual-channel water chiller designed for critical thinning, grinding, and scribing processes.

This innovative system features a highly integrated dual-water supply, delivering separate cooling streams for the spindle and the grinding interface, allowing semiconductor facilities to optimize floor space while streamlining production workflows.

Precision at Every Micron

Semiconductor thinning requires absolute thermal stability. TONFY’s chiller achieves an industry-leading ±0.1°C control accuracy across both key cooling channels:

  • Grinding & Scribing Water: Utilizing a DI water open-loop system to remove heat instantly, ensuring superior surface roughness and dimensional accuracy.
  • Spindle Cooling: Suppressing thermal deformation caused by high-speed rotation, which significantly reduces machining errors and equipment failure risks.

Copper-Free Design for High Purity

To meet the high-purity standards of modern cleanrooms, all water-side flow components feature a copper-free design . This eliminates the risk of copper ion contamination, while optional high-precision filtration ensures water quality and protects sensitive equipment, extending the system's service life.
