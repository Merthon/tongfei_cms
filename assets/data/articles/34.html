On July 15, 2025, TONFY was invited to attend the Asia Data Center Summit & Expo (Data Center Asia) in Hong Kong, a flagship event for the Asia-Pacific digital infrastructure industry.

Mr. Lv Yunbo, General Manager of TONFY’s Intelligent Computing Center Division, joined an elite roundtable featuring CTOs from the Global Computing Consortium (GCC), SLiquid Global, and GLP China. The panel focused on "Liquid Cooling Deployment and the ‘Double Zero Action’," addressing the soaring global demand for greener AI infrastructure.

Tackling Liquid Cooling Challenges

The forum addressed critical pain points in real-world data center environments, emphasizing TONFY’s practical experiences in overseas markets:

  • Secondary-Side System Optimization: Refining flow distribution and pressure control for server racks.
  • Material Compatibility: Evaluating the interaction between eco-friendly coolants and metals/seals to ensure long-term stability.
  • Quick Disconnect (QD) Reliability: Enhancing the safety and ease of maintenance for modular deployments.

The "Double Zero Action"

Mr. Lv detailed TONFY’s progress in the “Double Zero Action” —aiming for zero onsite assembly defects and zero deployment delays. By utilizing modular, prefabricated designs and pre-validating solutions at the factory, TONFY provides high-reliability liquid cooling that is essentially "plug-and-play."

Global Dialogue & Best Practices

"Participation in this summit marks an important step in our global engagement. We are committed to fostering an ecosystem that moves data centers toward higher efficiency and sustainable development."

By working closely with ecosystem partners worldwide, TONFY continues to promote the optimal implementation of liquid cooling technology, supporting the evolution of next-generation green data centers.
