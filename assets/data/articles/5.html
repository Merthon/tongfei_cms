Cutting-Edge Thermal Management Solutions

1. Centralized Cooling: One Unit Replaces Seven

  • +0.71 Efficiency Ratio: 400 kW central cooling station supports multiple containers; eliminates “one unit per container.”
  • Zero Heat Island: Four-sided intake, top exhaust design results in 15% lower energy consumption.
  • Cost Reduction: Seven small units integrated into one large system; sheet metal, piping, and electrical components reduced by 30%.
  • Maintenance: Detachable panels and low-speed top fan reduce maintenance effort and noise by 10 dB(A).

2. Full-Stack Liquid Cooling: Roof as Heat Exchanger

  • +5–8% Space Efficiency: PACK+PCS dual liquid-cooled system; main unit inside container, roof-mounted heat exchangers.
  • Efficiency Boost: Automatic natural cooling at ≤15°C increases annual efficiency by 30%.
  • Airflow Design: Top-to-top airflow reduces site heat islands; no side vents, minimal noise.

3. Immersion Cooling: “Hot Spring” for Cells

  • Safety & Efficiency: Cells fully immersed in non-flammable coolant; thermal resistance reduced by 50%, supporting >2C charge/discharge.
  • Proven Performance: 60 kW test showed energy consumption down 12% with an efficiency ratio of 3.48.
  • Fire Suppression: Coolant doubles as fire-suppression; thermal runaway time extended 10×, max ΔT <3°C, cell lifespan +15%.

4. Direct Cooling & Heating: One Machine, No Water

  • All-in-One: Refrigerant directly contacts cold plate; evaporation temperature +3–5°C, COP +12%.
  • Heat Pump: One-button heat pump replaces electric heating; low-temp energy use down 40%.
  • Reliability: No pumps or liquid piping; zero leakage risk, <30s response, maximized frequency regulation benefits.
