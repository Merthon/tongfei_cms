Tonfy recently had the honor of welcoming a delegation from AMADA, including Mr. Takahiro Yamanashi (President of AMADA Co., Ltd.), Mr. Dai Shiraishi (Chairman of AMADA China), and Mr. Takashi Furugori (General Manager of Beijing AMADA).

The visit provided an excellent opportunity to exchange insights and strengthen long-term collaboration. Tonfy has been a trusted partner of AMADA for years, supplying advanced temperature control solutions, including laser water-cooling machines.

In return, Tonfy has incorporated AMADA’s cutting-edge metal processing equipment into its operations, enhancing production capabilities and efficiency. Tonfy was also honored with the “Exemplary Smart Sheet Metal Manufacturing Plant” award by AMADA (China), recognizing its excellence in intelligent manufacturing.

Advanced equipment integrated into Tonfy's operations:

  • Press Brakes
  • Laser Cutting Machines
  • Fin Punches
  • Sheet Metal Presses

On-site Recognition

During the visit, the AMADA delegation toured Tonfy’s state-of-the-art production facilities, gaining first-hand insight into the company’s manufacturing processes, technological expertise, and R&D capabilities.

They commended Tonfy’s welding standards, AGV (Automated Guided Vehicle) systems , and end-to-end product development and delivery capabilities. The delegation also praised Tonfy’s continued investment in innovation and intelligent manufacturing.

Strategic Discussions

Following the tour, Tonfy’s General Manager, Mr. Haolei Zhang , shared the company’s market strategy and international expansion plans. The discussion covered global laser industry trends, technological advancements, and future collaboration opportunities.

Both parties agreed that Tonfy’s strategic direction aligns closely with AMADA’s business scope, offering significant potential for deeper cooperation.

Looking Ahead

This visit underscores more than a decade of successful collaboration and sets the stage for future growth.
