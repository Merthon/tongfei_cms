Ningde, China — On December 29, CATL (Contemporary Amperex Technology Co., Limited) held its 2023 Supplier Conference in Ningde, Fujian Province, under the theme "Zero-Carbon New Era, Ningde Gathering Core Strengths", honoring partners that made outstanding contributions to its high-quality development in 2023.

At the conference, TONFY was awarded the prestigious honor of "2023 Annual Excellent Supplier".

As one of CATL’s key partners, TONFY has long provided high-quality temperature control solutions and comprehensive services, delivering strong support for the development of CATL’s new energy business.

This recognition not only affirms TONFY’s products and services, but also validates its outstanding contributions in the field of new energy storage.
